What is hosting?

January 4, 2017

By Joe Ford

Hosting is the process of storing your website and making it visible to other people on the internet.

A website is a file, like a Word document, and needs to be stored somewhere. Back in the early 2000s it wasn’t unheard of for someone to make a website and save it to a folder on a regular computer. This computer would then run software which allowed people to access that folder over the internet.

So why do we need hosting companies?

There are many problems with storing everything on a regular computer. The first is that the computer storing the website must be turned on 24/7. If it’s switched off, users will find no reply and no site.

In the early days of the internet, there were limits on how much you could download which leads to another issue – if thousands of people connect to your computer through the internet, then it has to send a copy of the website to each of them through your internet connection. Without even noticing, you could break through your allowance and be subject to large charges. As websites became more and more complex, they started to become more than just a few files on a computer. Today, they are a complex collection of files, pictures, videos and instructions for the computer to follow requiring much more space and computing power meaning everyday computers are less suited to the task.

How do hosting companies help?

To get around these obstacles (and many more that I’ve not mentioned) people began to use hosting companies. These firms build specialised computers – or servers – that are designed to be on for 24 hours a day with backup power supplies in case of power cuts, special internet connections that allow high levels of uploads and downloads, and huge levels of computing power and storage space.

These companies could then rent parts of these servers to people who wanted to run a website. They also built interfaces so that people at home or in an office could send their websites to them. So when someone pays for hosting for their website, they’re actually paying to rent part of a huge computer in some part of the world to store it for them and to let other people access it.

Case Study - BEC

BEC Perimeter Security

We have helped B.E.C with many of our services including web design, web development, hosting, SEO and Google Adwords management. More details below.

GET STARTED

Web Design

BEC Perimeter Security came to Yuno looking for an overhaul on their website. Their previous website was pure html and was in need or modernisation.

The first step was to design a website that looked great for both BEC and their customers. BEC offer a range of services so it was important that all their visitors know what they have to offer when arriving on their website.

Web Development

The development of BEC was of medium complexity. With a large range of products and services an easy to navigate system needed to be created.

This development process involved mostly html, css and php.

Search Engine Optimisation

After launching the website we were then able to focus on optimising the website for search engines. In this case the main bulk of the process was on meta descriptions and Alt Text.
